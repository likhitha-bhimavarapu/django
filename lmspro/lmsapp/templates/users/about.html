About Us

Linkmein, is a Training and development and a software services company incorporated in Hyderabad. We have been providing Application Development & Managed Services, E-Commerce Solutions, BPO and Professional Training & Consulting Services in Hyderabad, India. We have been delivering services ranging from maintenance of existing applications to full life-cycle development of enterprise-wide business applications to the satisfaction of our customers competitively.
